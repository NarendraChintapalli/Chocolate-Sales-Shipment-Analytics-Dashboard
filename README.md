# 🍫 Chocolate Sales & Shipment Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze chocolate sales and shipment performance across different products, regions, and salespersons.

The dashboard transforms raw shipment data into actionable business insights through KPIs, trend analysis, geographic distribution, and performance tracking.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor overall sales and profitability.
* Analyze shipment volume and distribution.
* Identify top-performing products.
* Evaluate salesperson performance.
* Compare sales trends over time.
* Support business decision-making through interactive reporting.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Sales Trend Analysis

* Monthly sales comparison.
* Current Year vs Previous Year analysis.

### Shipment Distribution

* Shipment volume distribution across orders.
* Identification of shipment patterns.

### Product Performance

* Top 5 products by sales.
* Top 10 products by profitability.

### Geographic Analysis

* Sales contribution by country.
* Regional performance comparison.

### Salesperson Performance

* Ranking of top salespersons.
* Revenue and profit contribution analysis.

### Interactive Filtering

* Date range slicers.
* Dynamic dashboard exploration.
